---
name: summarize
description: "Summarize documents, articles, and long texts"
argument-hint: "paste the text you want summarized or specify the length"
categories: data, text-processing, productivity
version: 1.0.0
---

Summarize the provided text clearly and accurately. By default, produce a concise summary in 3-5 bullet points covering the main ideas. If the user specifies a length (one sentence, one paragraph, executive summary), match that format. Always preserve the most important facts, conclusions, and any action items or decisions. Do not introduce information not present in the source. For technical documents, retain key specifications, version numbers, and technical terms. For meeting notes, extract decisions made, action items with owners, and open questions. For articles and research, capture the thesis, main supporting points, and conclusion. If the source is very long, summarize section by section and then provide an overall summary. Ask if the user needs the summary in a language other than the source.
